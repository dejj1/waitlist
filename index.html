FAQs

Know more about FlexGig by checking out our frequently asked questions.

  • FlexGig is a freelance marketplace where freelancers can find remote job opportunities. Employers post job listings and freelancers can submit proposals for the jobs they are interested in. Once hired, freelancers can work remotely on their own schedule, based on the agreement with the client.

  • Sending proposals on FlexGig is completely free. Freelancers do not need to spend any connects or money to submit their proposals.

  • Yes, FlexGig offers a variety of courses for freelancers to upskill themselves. These courses can help freelancers enhance their skills and increase their chances of getting hired for better-paying projects.

  • FlexGig has career communities where freelancers can connect with other professionals in their industry. These communities are based on their profile and the services they offer. Freelancers can join the relevant community and engage in discussions, networking, and collaboration with other like-minded professionals.

  • FlexGig has a notifications and messages icon where freelancers can easily access any communications from their clients. They will be alerted about new messages and any updates regarding their job applications or ongoing projects.

  • FlexGig has a dedicated customer support page where freelancers can find information on how to get in touch with the support team. They can live chat, send a WhatsApp message, call a toll-free number, or send an email to support@flexgig.com for assistance.

  • Freelancers can apply for verification on FlexGig by providing their government-approved ID card, full name, email address, and undergoing a selfie verification process. Additionally, an OTP code will be sent to ensure that the verification process is completed by a real person rather than a bot.

  • Yes, FlexGig offers skill assessments similar to LinkedIn. These assessments are part of the verification process and help determine if freelancers are qualified and skilled in the areas they are applying for verification.

  • FlexGig provides verification badges for freelancers, agencies, and clients who have passed the necessary screening process. This helps streamline the hiring process and provides credibility to the parties involved. Additionally, AI interview questions may be used to assess freelancers' suitability for specific projects or roles.
